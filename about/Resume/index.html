ZHOU Zhang

First-principle-oriented Scientist with 8 years of research on the development of novel nanoscale devices in logic and memory.

Address: Institute of Physics (CAS), Beijing 100190
E-mail: zhouzhang1992@qq.com

Research

Transport researches in 2D materials, focused on magnetic-related interactions such as anomalous Hall effect and Kondo effect.

  • Skilled at nanofabrication, cryogenic measurements.
  • Lots of experience on mechanic exfoliation, pick-up techniques.
  • CVD growth of V5S8/ SnSe/ NbSe2.
